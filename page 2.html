About Disposable's:

Delta disposable products are typically items that are meant to be used once and then discarded. The term "Delta disposable" could refer to a specific brand or line of disposable products. These products are often convenient for single-use situations, reducing the need for cleaning or sterilization and minimizing the risk of contamination or spreading infections.
